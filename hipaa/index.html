HIPAA Privacy and Security Overview Training

BridgeFront’s new App, HIPAA Privacy and Security Training, features our award-winning online education material. This App is FREE for the first 100 downloads—after this, it is only $2.99.

About Bridgefront

BridgeFront is a trusted, fast growing company that believes in delivering high quality educational products and services to meet the growing needs of healthcare, government, business, and educational institutions. Our main differentiators are that we allow organizations the flexibility to create unique learning plans, edit our course content, build their own courses and improve staff competency levels.

Headquartered in Vancouver, WA, the firm started in 2002 with the most comprehensive online library available on HIPAA. Then, we added over 300+ titles in ICD-10, Regulatory Compliance, OSHA, Joint Commission and Revenue Cycle Management. Our Learning Management System or BridgeOne hosts an extensive online library of interactive, learner-focused courses.

Today, we stand a leader in the online education industry.
