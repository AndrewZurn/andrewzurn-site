A Little About Me...

My name is Andrew Zurn. I'm a developer, teacher, learner, do'er and achiever. I am currently working at The Nerdery as a Software Engineer on the JVM team. I recently graduated from Saint John's University (CSB|SJU) with a degree in Computer Science. I like to stay current on new technology, solve complex problems using new and different platforms and ideas, like to occasionally blog, and usually am sharing something interesting on Twitter.

I try to do things that make an impact and cause a difference. I would like to get more into open source, and I also like to share the knowledge that others have shared with me. I'm always teaching myself new things as well; it's my belief that learning should be a passion, and something everyone should experience everyday.

I'm still trying to find a place for myself in life, and although it may look like I have things figured out, trust me when I say, I have little of a clue as to what's actually going on or where I want to be.

About Me

I am a software developer with a few years of experience developing enterprise web and desktop applications. I'm a recent Johnnie graduate, and am now working at The Nerdery and also independently on a few personal projects. I'm largely a cross-platform developer, and my current focus is in the mobile space, but like to dabble in web development as well.
